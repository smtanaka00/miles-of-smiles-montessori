About Us

About Us

Miles of Smiles Montessori serves children from 6 months to 6 years in Bulawayo.

We follow authentic Montessori principles while adapting them to Zimbabwean life through African stories, outdoor learning, traditional music, and community values.

Our classrooms emphasize independence, concentration, respect, and joyful discovery.
